    • Careers

    • Interior Designer:

      • Create aesthetically pleasing and functional interior spaces for residential and commercial clients.

    • Commercial Interior Designer:

      • Specialize in designing interiors for commercial spaces such as offices, retail stores, and restaurants.

    • Residential Interior Designer:

      • Focus on designing interiors for homes, apartments, and other residential spaces.

    • Interior Architect:

      • Combine architectural knowledge with interior design skills to create cohesive and functional spaces.

    • Furniture Designer:

      • Design and create furniture pieces that complement the overall interior design concept.

    • Event and Exhibition Designer:

      • Create engaging and visually appealing designs for events, exhibitions, and trade shows.

    • Set Designer:

      • Work in the entertainment industry designing sets for films, TV shows, theater productions, and events.

    • Lighting Designer:

      • Specialize in designing lighting schemes that enhance the ambiance and functionality of interior spaces.

    • Color Consultant:

      • Provide expertise in selecting and coordinating colors to create harmonious and visually appealing interiors.

    • Interior Design Educator:

      • Teach interior design principles and techniques at educational institutions or through workshops and seminars.
