    • Careers

    • Hotel Manager:

      • Oversee the overall operations of a hotel, ensuring efficient and smooth functioning.

    • Front Office Manager:

      • Manage front desk operations, including guest check-in, reservations, and customer service.

    • Food and Beverage Manager:

      • Coordinate and oversee the food and beverage services within the hotel, including restaurants and catering.

    • Event Coordinator:

      • Plan and coordinate events hosted by the hotel, such as conferences, weddings, and meetings.

    • Housekeeping Manager:

      • Supervise and manage the housekeeping staff to ensure cleanliness and maintenance of guest rooms and common areas.

    • Revenue Manager:

      • Optimize hotel revenue through strategic pricing, inventory management, and sales strategies.

    • Human Resources Manager:

      • Handle HR functions within the hotel, including recruitment, training, and employee relations.

    • Marketing Manager:

      • Develop and implement marketing strategies to promote the hotel and attract guests.

    • Guest Services Manager:

      • Ensure high-quality guest services, addressing and resolving guest concerns and feedback.

    • Hotel Consultant:

      • Provide consulting services to hotels, offering expertise in management, operations, and improvement strategies.
