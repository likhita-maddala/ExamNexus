Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Mains Paper 2:

JEE Mains Paper 2 is conducted for admission to undergraduate programs in architecture and planning. It assesses candidates' aptitude in the field of architecture and evaluates their drawing and observation skills.

Key Details:

  • Exam Mode: Pen and Paper (Offline)
  • Subjects: Mathematics, Aptitude Test, Drawing Test
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Total Marks: 400
  • Official Website: JEE Main Official Website

For more details and to apply, please visit the official website of JEE Mains Paper 2.
