    • Event Planner:

    • Wedding Planner:

      • Specialize in planning and organizing weddings, including venue selection, catering, and decoration.

    • Corporate Event Coordinator:

      • Coordinate and execute corporate events, such as product launches, seminars, and team-building activities.

    • Exhibition Manager:

      • Organize and manage exhibitions, trade shows, and expos, ensuring smooth operations and participant satisfaction.

    • Event Marketing Specialist:

      • Combine event planning with marketing strategies to create and promote successful events.

    • Conference Organizer:

      • Plan and coordinate conferences, managing aspects such as speakers, schedules, and attendee experience.

    • Destination Wedding Planner:

      • Specialize in organizing weddings at specific destinations, handling logistics and travel arrangements.

    • Event Production Manager:

      • Oversee the technical and production aspects of events, including sound, lighting, and stage management.

    • Event Consultancy:

      • Provide consultancy services to businesses and individuals seeking guidance on event planning and management.
