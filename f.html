CA Final Overview:

The CA Final is the last level of the Chartered Accountancy course offered by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). It is a comprehensive examination covering advanced knowledge in financial reporting, strategic financial management, advanced auditing, and professional ethics, among other subjects.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: Financial Reporting, Strategic Financial Management, Advanced Auditing and Professional Ethics, Corporate and Economic Laws, Strategic Cost Management and Performance Evaluation, Elective Paper (one out of six options), Direct Tax Laws and International Taxation, Indirect Tax Laws.
  • Exam Format: Both groups can be taken together or separately
  • Mode: Pen and Paper (Offline)
  • Duration: Three hours for each paper
  • Official Website: ICAI Official Website

For detailed information about the CA Final examination, including syllabus, exam pattern, and study materials, please visit the official website of ICAI.
