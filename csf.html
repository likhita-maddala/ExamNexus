CS Foundation Overview:

The CS Foundation is the entry-level examination for the Company Secretaryship course offered by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). It is the first step in the CS journey, testing candidates on their basic understanding of subjects such as Business Environment and Law, Business Management, Ethics, and Communication.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: Business Environment and Law, Business Management, Ethics and Communication, Business Economics, Fundamentals of Accounting and Auditing.
  • Exam Format: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Mode: Online
  • Duration: Three hours for each paper
  • Official Website: ICSI Official Website

For detailed information about the CS Foundation examination, including syllabus, exam pattern, and study materials, please visit the official website of ICSI.
