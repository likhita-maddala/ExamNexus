    • Careers

    • Community Pharmacist:

      • Dispense medications, provide medication counseling, and offer health advice to individuals in a community pharmacy setting.

    • Hospital Pharmacist:

      • Work in a hospital setting, collaborating with healthcare teams to ensure safe and effective medication use for patients.

    • Industrial Pharmacist:

      • Contribute to the development, manufacturing, and quality control of pharmaceutical products in the industrial sector.

    • Research Scientist (Pharmaceutical Research):

      • Engage in pharmaceutical research to discover and develop new drugs, ensuring their safety and efficacy.

    • Regulatory Affairs Specialist:

      • Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements, submitting documentation for drug approvals and managing regulatory affairs.

    • Clinical Pharmacist:

      • Work directly with healthcare teams in clinical settings, providing specialized medication management for patients.

    • Pharmaceutical Sales Representative:

      • Promote and sell pharmaceutical products to healthcare professionals, pharmacies, and other stakeholders.

    • Academic Pharmacist (Educator/Researcher):

      • Teach pharmacy students, conduct research, and contribute to the academic and scientific community.

    • Consultant Pharmacist:

      • Provide expertise and advice on medication management, collaborating with healthcare facilities and organizations.

    • Pharmacy Informatics Specialist:

      • Manage and optimize the use of information technology in pharmacy practice, ensuring efficient and secure processes.
