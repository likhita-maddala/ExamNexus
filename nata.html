NATA Overview:

NATA is a national-level entrance examination in India for admission to undergraduate architecture programs. It assesses candidates' aptitude in drawing, observation skills, sense of proportion, aesthetic sensitivity, and critical thinking ability.

Key Details:

  • Exam Mode: Online (Part A) and Offline (Part B - Drawing Test)
  • Subjects: Mathematics, General Aptitude, Drawing
  • Duration: 3 hours (Part A - 1.5 hours, Part B - 1.5 hours)
  • Official Website: NATA Official Website

For detailed information about eligibility, exam pattern, and application process, please visit the official website of NATA.
