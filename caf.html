CA Foundation Overview:

The CA Foundation is the entry-level examination for the Chartered Accountancy course offered by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). It is designed to assess the knowledge and understanding of candidates in subjects such as Accounting, Business Laws, Economics, and Business Correspondence and Reporting.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: Principles and Practice of Accounting, Business Laws and Business Correspondence, Business Mathematics and Logical Reasoning & Statistics, Business Economics and Business and Commercial Knowledge.
  • Exam Format: Paper-based (Pen and Paper) and OMR Answer Sheets
  • Mode: Offline
  • Duration: Three hours for each paper
  • Official Website: ICAI Official Website

For detailed information about the CA Foundation examination, including syllabus, exam pattern, and study materials, please visit the official website of ICAI.
