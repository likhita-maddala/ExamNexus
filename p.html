Company Secretary (CS) Professional Examination:

The CS Professional Examination is the final level of the Company Secretaryship course conducted by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). It aims to assess the comprehensive knowledge and understanding of company secretarial practices.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: Various subjects covering advanced company law, corporate restructuring, governance, and more.
  • Exam Format: Combination of descriptive and objective questions
  • Duration: Multiple sessions spread across a few days
  • Official Website: ICSI Official Website

For detailed information, exam schedule, and study material, please visit the official website of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI).
