Overview

Exam Name:

Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Mains.

Purpose:

Entrance test for undergraduate engineering and architecture programs in India.

Conducting Body:

Organizer

: National Testing Agency (NTA).

Significance

Prerequisite for:

JEE Advanced.

Eligibility for Admission:

National Institutes of Technology (NITs), Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs), and other centrally funded technical institutions.

Exam Structure

Subjects Tested:

Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

Additional Papers:

Separate papers for B.Arch and B.Planning.

Frequency

Conducted Multiple Times a Year:

Provides flexibility and multiple opportunities for students. For more details, click on "official website"
