Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced:

JEE Advanced is the second stage of the JEE examination, and it is conducted for admission to the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). It is considered one of the most challenging engineering entrance exams in India.

Key Details:

  • Eligibility: Only candidates who qualify JEE Mains are eligible to appear for JEE Advanced.
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Exam Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Duration: 3 hours per paper (Paper 1 and Paper 2)
  • Official Website: JEE Advanced Official Website

For detailed information about eligibility, exam pattern, and application process, please visit the official website of JEE Advanced.
