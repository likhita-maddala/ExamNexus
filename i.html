CA Intermediate Overview:

The CA Intermediate level is the second level of the Chartered Accountancy course offered by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). It is a crucial stage in the CA journey, testing candidates on their in-depth knowledge and application of accounting principles, financial management, and other related subjects.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: Accounting, Corporate and Other Laws, Cost and Management Accounting, Taxation, Advanced Accounting, Auditing and Assurance, Enterprise Information Systems & Strategic Management, Financial Management & Economics for Finance.
  • Exam Format: Both groups can be taken together or separately
  • Mode: Pen and Paper (Offline)
  • Duration: Three hours for each paper
  • Official Website: ICAI Official Website

For detailed information about the CA Intermediate examination, including syllabus, exam pattern, and study materials, please visit the official website of ICAI.
