CS Executive Overview:

The CS Executive is the second level of the Company Secretaryship course offered by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI). It is a crucial stage in the CS journey, testing candidates on their advanced knowledge in company law, cost and management accounting, taxation, and other related subjects.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: Module 1 - Jurisprudence, Interpretation and General Laws, Company Law, Setting up of Business Entities and Closure, Tax Laws; Module 2 - Corporate and Management Accounting, Securities Laws and Capital Markets, Economic, Business and Commercial Laws.
  • Exam Format: OMR-based Examination (Module 1 and Module 2 can be taken together or separately)
  • Mode: Pen and Paper (Offline)
  • Duration: Three hours for each paper
  • Official Website: ICSI Official Website

For detailed information about the CS Executive examination, including syllabus, exam pattern, and study materials, please visit the official website of ICSI.
