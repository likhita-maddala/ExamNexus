AILET Overview:

AILET is a national-level entrance examination in India for admission to the National Law University, Delhi (NLU Delhi). It is conducted to select candidates for undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs in law.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: English, General Knowledge, Legal Aptitude, Reasoning, Elementary Mathematics (for UG); Constitutional Law, Jurisprudence, Criminal Law, Law of Contracts, etc. (for PG)
  • Exam Mode: Pen and Paper (Offline)
  • Duration: 1.5 hours (for UG); 2 hours (for PG)
  • Official Website: AILET Official Website

For detailed information about eligibility, exam pattern, and application process, please visit the official website of AILET.
