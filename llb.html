    • Careers

    • Lawyer:

      • Practice law by providing legal advice, representing clients, and handling legal disputes in various areas of law.

    • Corporate Counsel:

      • Work in-house for corporations, providing legal advice on business matters, contracts, and regulatory compliance.

    • Legal Consultant:

      • Offer expertise and advice on legal matters to individuals, businesses, or organizations on a contractual basis.

    • Judge:

      • Preside over legal cases in courts, making decisions based on the interpretation and application of the law.

    • Legal Researcher:

      • Conduct legal research to support lawyers, judges, or legal scholars in analyzing and understanding legal issues.

    • Public Prosecutor:

      • Represent the government in criminal cases, presenting evidence and arguments to secure convictions.

    • Legal Officer in Government:

      • Work for government agencies, providing legal advice, drafting legislation, and handling legal matters.

    • Legal Aid Attorney:

      • Provide legal assistance to individuals who cannot afford private representation, ensuring access to justice.

    • Legal Educator:

      • Teach law at educational institutions, imparting legal knowledge to future generations of lawyers.

    • Legal Entrepreneur:

      • Start and manage your own legal practice, consultancy, or legal-tech business.
