NEET Overview:

NEET is a national-level entrance examination in India for admission to undergraduate medical courses (MBBS/BDS) in government or private medical colleges. It is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

Key Details:

  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Botany and Zoology)
  • Exam Mode: Pen and Paper (Offline)
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Total Questions: 180
  • Negative Marking: Yes
  • Official Website: NEET Official Website

For detailed information about eligibility, exam pattern, and application process, please visit the official website of NEET.
