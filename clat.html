CLAT Overview:

CLAT is a national-level entrance examination in India for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate law programs offered by various law universities and colleges across the country. It assesses candidates' knowledge and aptitude in areas such as English, Legal Reasoning, Logical Reasoning, General Knowledge, and Mathematics.

Key Details:

  • Subjects: English Language, Current Affairs Including General Knowledge, Legal Reasoning, Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Techniques.
  • Exam Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Eligibility: For undergraduate and postgraduate law programs
  • Official Website: CLAT Official Website

For detailed information about eligibility, exam pattern, and application process, please visit the official website of CLAT.
