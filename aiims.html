AIIMS Overview:

AIIMS is a group of autonomous public medical colleges in India that operates under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. It is known for its high standards of medical education and research. AIIMS conducts entrance exams for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate medical courses in various disciplines.

Key Details:

  • Entrance Exams: AIIMS MBBS for undergraduate courses, AIIMS PG for postgraduate courses
  • Duration: Varies based on the course
  • Subjects: Various medical subjects based on the course
  • Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
  • Official Website: AIIMS Official Website

For detailed information about eligibility, exam pattern, and application process, please visit the official website of AIIMS.
